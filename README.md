## 🔑 Key Takeaway

> **AI is software.** Every pattern you see in agentic AI — fan-in/fan-out, pub/sub, retry with backoff, orchestration vs. choreography — comes from decades of distributed systems and cloud design patterns. The developers who thrive in AI are those who understand software engineering first.
